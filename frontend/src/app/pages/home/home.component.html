01
Objectif

An intelligent platform for managing urban waste

Our system centralizes all urban waste management in order to optimize collection routes, reduce costs, limit CO₂ emissions, and improve real-time container monitoring. It also facilitates the work of field agents and municipal services.

02
Features

Essential tools for modern management

The platform offers several key features: management of collection points (adding, editing, fill levels), management of vehicles and agents (assigned zones, availability), real-time interactive map, as well as intelligent route optimization based on distance, vehicle capacity, and the fill level of containers.

03
Advantages

Why adopt our solution?

The system reduces the time and fuel of collection routes, limits overflows, improves urban cleanliness and enables more intelligent resource management. Decisions are made based on real data, ensuring a more efficient and sustainable organization.

04
Impact

Commitment to the environment and sustainability

By optimizing routes and reducing CO₂ emissions, our system directly contributes to environmental preservation. Each intelligent collection is a step towards a cleaner and more sustainable city. We measure and report the environmental impact of each operation.
